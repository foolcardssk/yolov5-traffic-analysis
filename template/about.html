About Us

Welcome to our website! We are a team of developers passionate about computer vision and video analysis. Our goal is to provide efficient solutions for tracking and analyzing objects in videos.

Tracking Algorithm

    We have developed a custom object tracking algorithm using computer vision techniques. The algorithm uses the YOLOv5 model for object detection and tracking. It utilizes the OpenCV library for image processing and the PyTorch framework for deep learning. The algorithm is designed to track objects in real-time, providing accurate and reliable results.

Key Features
  • Precise Object Tracking: Our algorithm detects and tracks multiple objects simultaneously, assigning unique IDs to each.
  • Traffic Density Analysis: Define specific areas of interest in videos to analyze traffic density levels (high, medium, or low).
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our interface allows easy interaction, visualization of tracking results, and export of analysis data.
